About Us

Our Mission

At FITAHI Clinic, our mission is to provide accessible, reliable, and affordable mental health solutions to the people of Kenya. We are dedicated to creating a safe, confidential, and non-judgmental space where individuals, couples, and families can find healing and growth. We believe that mental well-being is a cornerstone of a fulfilling life, and we are committed to being your trusted partner on your journey to wellness.

Our Vision

We envision a community where mental health is prioritized, and seeking help is a sign of strength. Our goal is to destigmatize mental health care in Nairobi and beyond, fostering a culture of compassion, understanding, and resilience. Through our expert care and community engagement, we aim to empower our clients to lead healthier, happier lives.

Our Values

Compassion

We approach every client with empathy, kindness, and a deep understanding of their unique struggles.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards, ensuring confidentiality, professionalism, and trust in all our interactions.

Growth

We are committed to the personal and emotional growth of our clients, helping them build resilience and achieve their full potential.

Dr. Neema Araka MD, Psychiatrist, Founder of FITAHI Clinic
