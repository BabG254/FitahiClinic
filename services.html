Our Services

Psychiatry

Our psychiatric services involve a comprehensive approach to diagnosing and treating a wide range of mental health conditions. We provide expert assessment and evidence-based treatment for issues such as anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, insomnia, and more. Our psychiatrists work collaboratively with clients to develop personalized treatment plans, which may include medication management, lifestyle adjustments, and therapeutic interventions to promote lasting mental wellness.

Psychotherapy

Psychotherapy, or talk therapy, is a powerful tool for personal growth and healing. At FITAHI Clinic, our skilled therapists provide a supportive environment to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. We specialize in addressing challenges such as grief and loss, identity crisis, trauma, and stress management. Through various therapeutic modalities, we help you develop coping strategies, improve self-awareness, and build a more resilient mindset.

Book a Psychotherapy Session

Couples & Family Counselling

Relationships are at the heart of our well-being, but they can also be a source of conflict and stress. Our couples and family counselling services are designed to help you navigate challenges, improve communication, and strengthen your bonds. We provide a neutral and supportive space to address issues related to separation, divorce, parenting, and other relational dynamics. Our goal is to help you build healthier, more fulfilling relationships.
